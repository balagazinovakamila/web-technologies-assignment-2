Coco Chanel

1883 - 1971

Task 1 Task 2 Task 3 Task 4

Coco Chanel (1883-1971) was a French fashion designer and founder of the Chanel brand. She revolutionized women's clothing by introducing elegance with comfort. Chanel is famous for the little black dress, Chanel No. 5 perfume, and timeless suits. She remains one of the most influential figures in fashion history.

Important Events

  1. 1883 - Born in Saumur, France
  2. 1910 - Opened her first boutique in Paris
  3. 1921 - Released Chanel No. 5 perfume
  4. 1925 - Introduced the Chanel suit
  5. 1971 - Passed away in Paris

Key Contributions

  • Little Black Dress (LBD)
  • Chanel No. 5 perfume
  • Women's suits and trousers
  • Luxury jewelry and handbags
